Overview

I worked as a front-end developer for Camp K12, an edtech platform that helps students learn through interactive projects. My main task was to develop a new HatchXR Dashboard to make the platform more competitive and engaging by showcasing user projects and progress.

Frontend Design

I collaborated closely with UI/UX designer to ensure the dashboard was visually appealing and intuitive. Additionally, I worked with the main backend developer to seamlessly integrate the front-end design with backend functionalities, bringing the HatchXR Dashboard to life. My role focused on translating designs into responsive, interactive, and user-friendly web components while ensuring smooth data flow between the front end and back end.

Developed a user-friendly dashboard home page to streamline access to HatchXR projects, leaderboards, and learning resources.
