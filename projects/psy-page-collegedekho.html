Overview

While working at CollegeDekho, an ed-tech company that helps students and parents find the right college, I developed a psychometric page designed to guide students toward their ideal career paths. This project included a main landing page and a multi-step quiz for career profiling. The goal was to create a seamless and engaging user experience for students exploring their career options.

Frontend Design

I independently managed all front-end development, ensuring the design was fully responsive and easy to navigate on both desktop and mobile devices. Below are screenshots showcasing the design and functionality of the psychometric page, including the main landing page and the multi-step career profiling quiz.

What I Did

  • Landing Page Design: Built the main landing page, focusing on an intuitive design that appealed to students and parents.
  • Multi-Step Quiz: Developed a career profiling quiz that guided users through a series of questions and provided personalized career suggestions.
  • Validation and Report Generation: Integrated backend validation to ensure the accuracy of responses and created functionality to generate detailed career reports based on quiz answers.
  • Responsive Development: Ensured the psychometric page worked smoothly on both mobile and desktop devices.

What I Learned

This project allowed me to combine my technical skills with an understanding of user needs in the education sector. Helping students discover their career paths made this experience both meaningful and enriching.
